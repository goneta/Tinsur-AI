Tinsur.AI Eighth Backend Milestone Validation

Scope
-----
Milestone 8 implements automatic premium schedule orchestration for product-catalog policy acquisition. The acquisition flow can now generate first-class premium schedule rows immediately after an approved product-catalog quote is automatically issued as a policy. The response includes schedule status, installment metadata, and aggregate premium schedule summary information.
