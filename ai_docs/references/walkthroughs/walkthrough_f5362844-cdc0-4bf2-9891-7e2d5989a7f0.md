# Walkthrough: Core Lifecycle Verification

This walkthrough documents the successful verification of the core insurance lifecycle: **Quote -> Policy -> Payment -> Claim**.

## Verification Overview

I created and ran a comprehensive verification script `backend/app/scripts/verify_lifecycle.py` that exercises the backend services and repositories to ensure the fundamental business logic is sound.

### Verification Steps & Results

| Step | Action | Status | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Client Creation** | ✅ Success | New client `Test Lifecycle` created. |
| 2 | **Quote Creation** | ✅ Success | Quote generated with AI premium calculation. |
| 3 | **Quote Acceptance** | ✅ Success | Quote status updated to `accepted`. |
| 4 | **Policy Conversion** | ✅ Success | Quote successfully converted to active Policy. |
| 5 | **Schedule Generation** | ✅ Success | 12-month premium schedule automatically generated. |
| 6 | **Payment Processing** | ✅ Success | Installment paid and schedule updated. |
| 7 | **Claim Submission** | ✅ Success | Claim linked to policy and submitted. |

## Key Findings

- **Lifecycle Integrity**: The end-to-end flow is functional at the data and service level.
- **Auto-Generation**: Premium schedules and policy numbers are being generated correctly.
- **Model Consistency**: I fixed a minor `TypeError` in the initial script where the `Client` model required a `client_type` field and used `user_id` instead of `created_by`.
