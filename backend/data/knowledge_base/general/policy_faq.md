# Tinsur.AI Policy FAQ & Coverage Details

## Policies Overview
Tinsur.AI offers three main tiers of auto insurance: Bronze, Silver, and Gold.

### Bronze Plan
- **Liability Coverage**: Up to $50,000 for bodily injury and $25,000 for property damage.
- **Collision Deductible**: $1,000.
- **Comprehensive Deductible**: $1,000.
- **Roadside Assistance**: Not included.
- **Windshield Replacement**: Not covered.
- **Rental Reimbursement**: Not included.

### Silver Plan
- **Liability Coverage**: Up to $100,000 for bodily injury and $50,000 for property damage.
- **Collision Deductible**: $500.
- **Comprehensive Deductible**: $500.
- **Roadside Assistance**: Included (limit 3 events per year).
- **Windshield Replacement**: Included with $50 deductible.
- **Rental Reimbursement**: Up to $30/day for max 30 days.

### Gold Plan
- **Liability Coverage**: Up to $500,000 for bodily injury and $100,000 for property damage.
- **Collision Deductible**: $250.
- **Comprehensive Deductible**: $250.
- **Roadside Assistance**: Unlimited events.
- **Windshield Replacement**: Included with $0 deductible (Free).
- **Rental Reimbursement**: Up to $50/day for max 45 days.
- **Gap Insurance**: Included.
- **New Car Replacement**: Included if totaled within first 2 years.

## Common Questions

### How do I file a claim?
You can file a claim directly through the chat by saying "I want to file a claim". You will need to provide photos of the vehicle and the incident date. Our AI Claims Agent will analyze the damage immediately.

### What is Telematics?
Telematics uses your smartphone sensors to track driving habits like braking, acceleration, and cornering. A good driving score (>85) can earn you up to a 20% discount on your renewal premium.

### Can I share my policy with my spouse?
Yes, you can add a driver to your policy. Use the chat command "Update my policy" or contact support. To share the actual document, ask the Document Agent "Share my policy document".

### How are premiums calculated?
Premiums are based on vehicle value, driver age, location, and your Telematics Driving Score.

### Improving your Driving Score
To improve your score, avoid hard braking and rapid acceleration. Do not use your phone while driving. Maintain a steady speed.
