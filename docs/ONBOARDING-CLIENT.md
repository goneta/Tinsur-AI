## Getting Started

### Welcome to Tinsur-AI

Tinsur-AI is a modern, cloud-based insurance management platform designed to streamline client management, quote creation, and policy tracking. Whether you're new to digital insurance management or migrating from legacy systems, this guide will help you get up and running in minutes.

### System Requirements

- **Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version)
- **Internet:** Reliable broadband connection
- **Device:** Desktop, laptop, or tablet (mobile optimization available)
- **Passwords:** Secure password (minimum 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)

### Account Setup

#### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the Tinsur-AI login page at your organization's dedicated URL
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Create New Account"**
3. Enter your:
   - Full name
   - Business email address
   - Company name
   - Password (strong recommendation: 12+ characters)
4. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. Check your email for a verification link (check spam folder if needed)
7. Click the verification link to activate your account

#### Step 2: Set Up Your Organization Profile

After email verification, you'll be prompted to complete your organization profile:

- **Organization Name:** Your company's official name
- **Industry Type:** Select insurance agency, broker, or management firm
- **Location:** Your primary business address
- **Phone Number:** Main contact number
- **Logo:** Upload your company logo (optional, but recommended)
- **Brand Colors:** Customize dashboard appearance (optional)

#### Step 3: Dashboard Orientation

Once logged in, you'll see your main dashboard with:

**Quick Stats:**
- Total Clients
- Active Policies
- Pending Quotes

**Recent Activity:**
- Latest client interactions
- Unread messages
- Tasks needing attention

**Main Navigation:**
- **Dashboard** — Overview and quick stats
- **Clients** — Manage all client records
- **Quotes** — Create and manage insurance quotes
- **Policies** — Track active and expired policies
- **Reports** — Generate business analytics
- **Team** — Manage user roles and permissions
- **Settings** — Organization and account settings

### Navigation Tips

- **Search Bar:** Quickly find clients, quotes, or policies
- **Notifications Bell:** Real-time alerts for important events
- **Profile Menu:** Account settings and preferences
- **Breadcrumbs:** Navigate back easily

## Step-by-Step Tutorials

### Tutorial 1: Create Your First Client Profile

**Time Required:** 3 minutes

1. Click **"Clients"** in the left navigation
2. Click **"+ Add New Client"**
3. Fill required fields:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email
   - Phone
4. Add optional details:
   - Company name
   - Address
   - Tags
5. Click **"Save Client"**

**Pro Tip:** Use tags like "VIP", "Renewal Q2", "Referred" to organize your clients.

### Tutorial 2: Create an Insurance Quote

**Time Required:** 5-7 minutes

1. Navigate to **"Quotes"**
2. Click **"+ Create New Quote"**
3. **Select Client** from dropdown
4. **Choose Coverage Type:**
   - Property Insurance
   - Liability Insurance
   - Commercial Auto
   - Workers' Compensation
   - Specialty Coverage
5. **Enter Risk Details** for your situation
6. **Review AI-Generated Quote**
7. **Customize Coverage:**
   - Adjust deductible
   - Select coverage limits
   - Add optional riders
8. **Set Quote Details:**
   - Expiration date
   - Assigned agent
9. **Route for Approval** or **Send to Client**

### Tutorial 3: Manage Policies

**Time Required:** 5 minutes

1. Go to **"Policies"**
2. **Filter by Status:**
   - Active
   - Expiring Soon
   - Expired
3. **View Policy Details** by clicking any policy
4. **Take Actions:**
   - Renew Policy
   - Update Coverage
   - Add Documents
   - Track Claims
5. **Receive Reminders** for renewals

### Tutorial 4: Generate Reports

**Time Required:** 3-5 minutes

1. Navigate to **"Reports"**
2. **Select Report Type:**
   - Client Summary
   - Premium Revenue
   - Policy Status
   - Quote Conversion Rate
   - Custom Report
3. **Set Filters:**
   - Date range
   - Client segment
   - Coverage type
4. **Generate and Download** as CSV or PDF

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

### Common Issues

**Problem: I Forgot My Password**
- Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Enter your email address
- Click reset link in email
- Create a new, strong password

**Problem: Client Not Found**
- Search for similar names in Clients
- Create new client if not found
- Then create quote

**Problem: Dashboard Loading Slowly**
- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many clients can I manage?**
A: Unlimited. Tinsur-AI scales to any size.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes. Create custom reports and export as CSV or PDF.

**Q: What happens if a quote expires?**
A: Expired quotes become read-only. Create a new quote.

**Q: Can I customize templates?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Quote Templates.

**Q: How do I track client interactions?**
A: Use the Notes field on each client record.

**Q: Can I integrate with other systems?**
A: Yes. Contact support for integration setup.
