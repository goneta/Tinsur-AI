## For Insurance Companies & Carriers

This guide is designed for insurance company administrators and underwriting teams.

### Quick Start

1. **Create Account** at your dedicated URL
2. **Complete Organization Profile** with company details
3. **Invite Team Members** with appropriate roles
4. **Configure Integration** with your existing systems
5. **Start Accepting Quotes** from partner agents

### Key Features for Insurance Companies

- **Quote Management System** — Receive and process quotes from partners
- **Underwriting Workflow** — Review, approve, or decline quotes
- **Policy Issuance** — Generate policy documents automatically
- **Claims Management** — Track and manage customer claims
- **Reporting & Analytics** — Monitor portfolio and performance
- **Integration Capability** — Connect with existing systems

### Team Setup

Create team members with these roles:

- **Underwriter** — Review and approve/decline quotes
- **Operations** — Issue policies, manage documents
- **Claims Administrator** — Handle claims processing
- **Report Manager** — Generate analytics and reports
- **System Administrator** — Manage users and settings

### Core Workflows

1. **Receive Quote** → Agent submits via platform
2. **Underwrite** → Review risk and approve
3. **Issue Policy** → Generate documents
4. **Manage** → Track renewals and claims
5. **Report** → Generate analytics
